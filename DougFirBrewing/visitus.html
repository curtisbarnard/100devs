The Best Beer Wins Out

We are always changing up the beer we have on tap, but we keep the beer with the most sales consistently on tap so you can come back to an old standby every time you visit. Plus the longer a keg is on tap, the lower the price goes to help influence only having the freshest beer available.

Tasty local food

We have a weekly selection of charcuterie boards and snacks to pair ith your beer. We source the meat, cheese and bread locally too! If you're feeling hungrier you are welcome to bring in your own food or grab something from our neighbors.

See where and how it's made

You can see directly into our brewing deck as you sip a pint Fir Needle IPA. If you want to get closer, we also offer tours once a day that are 30 minutes long and offer a behind the scenes look and ability to taste some exclusive beer. Spots are first come first serve to those at our brewery.
