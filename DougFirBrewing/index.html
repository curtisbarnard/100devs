Fresh Beer

We aren't held down by styles, instead we brew what we like to drink depending on the season and ingredients available.

Premium local hops

We get only the best hops direct from Bobsby Hops located in Woodburn, OR.

Only the purest water

Our water comes from the world famous Bull Run Watershed. It's the cleanest, tastiest water coming directly from the slopes of Mt. Hood

Eastern Oregon Barley

We source barley from east of the Cascades where the weather is perfect for growing tasty golden fields of barley. The barley is then malted at a local facility in Vancouver, Washington.

Small Batch, Local Yeast

Our yeast comes from our friends over at Rose City Yeast, A small batch, local producer of the finest yeast this side of the Mississippi!
